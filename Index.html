What is Bigha?
▼
Bigha is a traditional unit used across many states in India. Its size varies widely by region — e.g., ~6,800 sq.ft in some states to ~27,225 sq.ft in others; always choose your state for accurate conversions.
What is Decimal?
▼
Decimal commonly denotes 1/100 of an acre (0.01 acre). Widely used in West Bengal & some Eastern states.
How precise are these conversions?
▼
This app uses commonly cited per-state conversion values. District-level differences may exist. For legal uses, consult the state revenue office or official portal linked above.
